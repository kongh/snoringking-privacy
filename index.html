呼噜大王 - 隐私政策

最后更新：2026年3月8日

概述

呼噜大王（"本应用"）非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了我们如何处理您使用本应用时的信息。

数据收集与存储

  • 所有录音数据仅存储在您的设备本地，我们不会上传任何音频文件到云端服务器。
  • 打鼾检测与睡眠分析完全在设备端通过机器学习模型完成，不涉及任何网络传输。
  • 我们不会收集、存储或共享您的个人身份信息。

麦克风权限

本应用需要访问您的麦克风以录制夜间睡眠声音，用于检测打鼾、说梦话、咳嗽等声音事件。录音仅在您主动启动录音功能时进行，且录音数据仅保存在您的设备上。

订阅服务

本应用提供订阅服务（呼噜大王 Pro）。订阅由 Apple 管理，付款通过您的 App Store 账户处理。我们不会收集或存储您的支付信息。订阅管理和续订由 Apple 按照其条款和政策处理。

第三方服务

  • RevenueCat：用于管理订阅状态。RevenueCat 可能会收集匿名的订阅相关数据。详见 RevenueCat 隐私政策。

数据删除

您可以随时在应用设置中删除所有录音数据和睡眠记录。卸载应用将自动删除所有本地数据。

儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

隐私政策变更

我们可能会不定期更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在此页面发布，并更新"最后更新"日期。

Snoring King - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Overview

Snoring King ("the App") takes your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Data Collection and Storage

  • All recording data is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload any audio files to cloud servers.
  • Snoring detection and sleep analysis are performed entirely on-device using machine learning models, with no network transmission involved.
  • We do not collect, store, or share your personally identifiable information.

Microphone Permission

The App requires access to your microphone to record nighttime sleep sounds for detecting snoring, sleep talking, coughing, and other sound events. Recording only occurs when you actively start the recording function, and all recording data is stored solely on your device.

Subscription Service

The App offers a subscription service (Snoring King Pro). Subscriptions are managed by Apple, and payments are processed through your App Store account. We do not collect or store your payment information. Subscription management and renewals are handled by Apple in accordance with its terms and policies.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat: Used to manage subscription status. RevenueCat may collect anonymous subscription-related data. See the RevenueCat Privacy Policy for details.

Data Deletion

You can delete all recording data and sleep records at any time through the App's settings. Uninstalling the App will automatically delete all local data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
